Bridging Languages with Generative Models: Advances in Speech Technology

Sponsored by IEEE Signal Processing Society and Department of CSE, IIT Indore

Date: December 15-16, 2025

Location: Sandipani Seminar Hall, IIT Indore

About

This two-day workshop aims to bring together researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to explore the latest advancements in Signal Processing, Artificial Intelligence, and Speech Technologies. It will feature expert talks and interactive discussions designed to offer deeper insights into cutting-edge methodologies and real-world applications. Participants will have the opportunity to learn about emerging trends, ongoing research challenges, and innovative solutions shaping the future of these domains.

In addition to technical sessions, the workshop will provide a platform for meaningful interaction with leading experts, encouraging the exchange of ideas and fostering collaboration between academia and industry. Whether you are an early-career researcher, a student, or an experienced professional, this workshop aims to enrich your knowledge, spark new research directions, and strengthen connections within the scientific community.
